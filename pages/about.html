// about hack2own

We think the surface should be free.

Hack2Own started with a simple frustration: the best cybersecurity training is locked behind a paywall before you even know if the field is for you. So we flipped it. The introduction to every topic — the L0 surface — is free forever. You only pay when you decide to go deeper.

Born in Kerala, built for India

There's an enormous amount of curiosity about offensive security here, and very few hands-on, affordable, structured ways into it. We're building Hack2Own for the student in Kochi, the IT support engineer in Trivandrum, and everyone across India who wants to become a real penetration tester — not just collect certificates.

We start at the surface and go pan-India from there.

Depth, not breadth

Four levels per module — L0 to L3 — so you can descend exactly as far as your goals require, from curious beginner to OSCP candidate.

Cert-aligned

Each level points at an industry benchmark: CEH at L1, eJPT at L2, OSCP at L3. You always know what you're training toward.

Ethics first

Everything we teach is for authorised, legal testing. We build the legal mindset in from L0, because skills without ethics are a liability.

Affordable on purpose

Priced for Indian learners — one-time options, à la carte modules, and subscriptions that get cheaper per-module the deeper you commit.
